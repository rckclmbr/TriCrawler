If you are looking to get one of the most aerodynamic frames on the markets at a fraction of the price then the Felt B16 triathlon bike is it! The Felt B16 is Felt's most affordable full carbon TT/Triathlon bike and is designed with the same mold as the legendary Felt DA. With its carbon frame and fork, riders get carbon fiber's endurance-enhancing vibration damping. A mix of Shimano and FSA components maximize performance and value in a reliable package while the deep section wheels offer real-world aero benefits.

The Ride: The Felt B16 triathlon bike is one of the best values on the market today in terms of performance, aerodynamics, weight, and components. All the same great ride qualities that you would expect in the Felt B series. It's comfortable, stiff, beautiful, need we say more?

Click here to watch a video about the numerous features the Felt B16 triathlon bike has to offer!

Features/Benefits:
  • Same frame mold as the Felt DA
  • High modulus (HM) carbon fiber frame
  • Internal cable routing, with full length housing (one of the best designs on the market!)
  • Rear wheel fairing, with rear brake hidden behind the bottom bracket
  • Dual Position Carbon Aero Seatpost
  • Shimano 105 20 speed, Felt TTR-4 wheels
  • Felt 3.3 Team Issue, Carbon Base, Waterproof saddle, Cromoly rails
  • 20.72 lbs.
