Sampson Sports Stratics TT Carbon Aerobar
The Sampson Stratics TT carbon aerobar is Sampson's aerobar flagship and is definitely turning some heads in the triathlon market! Just take one look and you start to see why! It features an extremely minimal front profile, which you can see in the inset. Of course being aerodynamic is only part of the story. In order for any aerobar to be effective it needs to be comfortable enough so that the user will spend the majority of their time in the aero-position. How do you create a comfortable integrated aerobar? Adjustability, comfortable cups, and ergonomic extensions are key to comfort, all of which Sampson has addressed with the Stratics carbon aerobar. Rotational and fore/aft adjustment of the extensions and the armrest cups will enable you to get the best fit possible. Ergonomic extensions which keep your hands comfortable and pain free even into the later stages in a training day or race.

Specifications:
  • Base bar has stealth fighter aero-shaping, but offers a super-stiff design for climbing power when on the horns or pulling hard on the extensions. Grooved hand grips on the base bar.
  • Extensions are available in low-rise (CHICANE) or ski tip (SKITIP) style and offer 50 mm fore-aft adjustment, and unlimited rotational adjustment with a simple collet system
  • Extensions feature full ergo shaping at ends to keep your hands open and relaxed
  • Full alloy arm rests are stiff to provide superior steering input, keep forearms from rolling off the supports. Armrests are also super adjustable for width, angle and fore-aft position. Riding a precise line can literally shave miles off a 112 mi. IM event!
  • All cable routing is internal for better aerodynamics, crisp looks.
  • Textured grip surface is behind the brake and shifter area and is part of the finish
  • Bars use a standard 31.8 diameter stem for easy fitting for height and reach
